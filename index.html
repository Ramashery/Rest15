Truffle Ravioli

€28

Our signature dish, the Truffle Ravioli, is a testament to Italian culinary artistry. Handcrafted pasta pockets are filled with a luxurious blend of ricotta, parmigiano reggiano, and black truffle from Alba. Tossed in a delicate brown butter and sage sauce, each bite reveals layers of earthy aroma and creamy texture. The dish is finished with freshly shaved white truffle and a drizzle of truffle oil, creating an unforgettable symphony of flavors that lingers on the palate.

Ingredients

  • Fresh handmade pasta
  • Alba black truffle
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Brown butter sauce
  • Fresh sage

Pairing Recommendations

  • Barolo Riserva 2015
  • Chianti Classico
  • Pinot Nero

Chef's Notes

  • Best enjoyed immediately
  • Inspired by Northern Italian traditions
  • Features seasonal truffles
